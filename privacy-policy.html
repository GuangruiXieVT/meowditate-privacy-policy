Meowditate Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Meowditate respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you register or subscribe, for the sole purpose of associating your account with your subscription and providing essential account support.
  • Local Data: Your personal data, including mood logs and chat data, are stored on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and link your account to your subscription.
  • To send essential service notifications related to your account or subscription (e.g., expiration reminders).

We do not use your email address for advertising or marketing. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Payments and Subscriptions

Our app uses Google Play Billing for subscription management. All subscription transactions are processed securely by Google Play, and we do not have access to your payment details.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your email is stored only for as long as your account and subscription are active. You can request deletion of your email and account by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Play services. For more information, please see Google Play's Privacy Policy.
